# VNG Corporation — Q3 2024 Performance Snapshot

**Source**: Internal research note (synthetic seed for autonomous-researcher demo).
**Date**: 2024-11-15

VNG Corporation, Vietnam's largest internet company by revenue, reported third-quarter 2024 revenue of approximately VND 2,400 billion, up roughly 12% year-on-year. Operating segments include online games (Zalo Pay Games, Crossfire Legends), digital payments (ZaloPay), enterprise cloud (VNG Cloud) and Zalo messaging.

## Highlights

- **Online games**: Continued to be the largest revenue contributor (~55% of total).
- **ZaloPay**: Monthly active users surpassed 14 million; transaction volume up ~30% YoY but the segment remained loss-making due to acquisition spend.
- **VNG Cloud**: Revenue up 40% YoY thanks to enterprise wins in BFSI and retail; still sub-scale vs. AWS Vietnam and Viettel IDC.
- **AI investments**: Released Vietnamese-language LLM "VinaLLaMA" research preview and integrated GenAI features into Zalo's customer-care suite.

## Strategic posture

VNG continues to position itself as a "super-app + cloud + AI" play, mirroring the strategies of regional peers like Sea Group and Grab. Management reiterated guidance for full-year 2024 revenue of VND 9,000-9,500 billion.

## Key risks

1. ZaloPay competition with MoMo (which has >30M MAU) and VNPay.
2. Regulatory pressure on online games (loot box guidelines, Decree 71).
3. FX exposure on USD-denominated cloud infrastructure costs.
