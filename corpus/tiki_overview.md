# Tiki Corporation — Company Overview

**Source**: Internal research note (synthetic seed).
**Date**: 2024-09-20

Tiki is one of Vietnam's "big four" e-commerce platforms alongside Shopee, Lazada, and Sendo. Founded in 2010 by Tran Ngoc Thai Son, Tiki has positioned itself as the "trusted, fast-shipping" alternative — emphasising authenticity guarantees and 2-hour TikiNOW delivery in HCMC and Hanoi.

## Financial trajectory

- 2020-2022: aggressive growth funded by Series E (USD 258M led by AIA, Yuanta, Mirae Asset, 2021).
- 2023: capital efficiency pivot; market share contracted vs. Shopee, which used Sea Group's deeper pockets.
- 2024 H1: reported a return to gross-margin profitability on a unit-economics basis; net loss narrowed materially.

## AI strategy

Tiki's AI investments have been pragmatic and focused on:
1. **Search & discovery**: re-ranking with learned-to-rank models tuned on Vietnamese queries.
2. **Catalogue moderation**: visual + NLP models to detect counterfeit listings (a key brand differentiator).
3. **Customer service**: a Vietnamese GenAI chatbot launched in mid-2024 handles ~40% of L1 inquiries.

Compared with VNG (which builds horizontal AI capabilities including a base LLM), Tiki is much more vertical/applied — reusing open-source models rather than developing foundation models in-house.

## Outlook

Tiki is widely expected to pursue an IPO once Vietnamese regulatory conditions allow (likely Singapore listing rather than HOSE). Strategic discussions with regional players (Sea, Bukalapak, Tokopedia/GoTo) have been reported in industry press but no transaction has been confirmed.
