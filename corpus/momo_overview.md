# MoMo (M_Service JSC) — Company Overview

**Source**: Internal research note (synthetic seed).
**Date**: 2024-10-30

MoMo (operated by M_Service Joint Stock Company) is Vietnam's leading e-wallet, with more than 30 million monthly active users as of mid-2024. The company achieved unicorn status in late 2021 after a USD 200M Series E round led by Mizuho Bank, valuing it at over USD 2 billion.

## Business lines

- **Personal payments** (P2P, bill-pay, top-up): the original product, still the largest user funnel.
- **Merchant acquiring**: ~250,000 merchants accept MoMo QR; integrated with major retailers (Circle K, Phuc Long, GS25).
- **Financial services marketplace**: insurance distribution, mutual funds, micro-loans through partners.
- **GenAI assistant**: launched MoMo AI in mid-2024, a Vietnamese-language financial assistant.

## AI strategy

MoMo has prioritised three GenAI use-cases: (1) personalised recommendations of financial products, (2) Vietnamese-language conversational support, (3) fraud-detection scoring on transactions. Internal models are tuned on top of open-source bases (Llama 3, Qwen 2) rather than proprietary training from scratch.
